Features

Everything a team needs.

Built for engineers who want clarity over chaos — from solo side projects to cross-functional sprints.

⬡

Dependency mapping

Visualize task relationships with an interactive dependency graph. Automatically detect circular dependencies before they cause delays.

◎

Monte Carlo simulation

Run risk simulations across thousands of scenarios. Get confidence intervals for delivery dates instead of wishful estimates.

⚡

AI scheduling

AI-assisted prioritization surfaces the highest-impact tasks. Smart deadline suggestions based on team velocity and task complexity.

⊞

Multiple views

Switch between Kanban, list, and timeline layouts. Adapts to Agile, Shape Up, Waterfall, and Kanban workflows out of the box.

◈

Real-time collaboration

Live updates, inline comments, and @mentions. Role-based access control with granular permissions per project and workspace.

⊡

Progress analytics

Burndown charts, velocity tracking, and custom reports. Exportable metrics for stakeholder updates and retrospectives.

How it works

Plan smarter, ship faster.

From goal to execution in minutes — not a week of Jira config.

01

Define your goal

Enter a high-level objective. Nexus breaks it into tasks, estimates scope, and flags ambiguous requirements.

02

Map dependencies

Drag to connect tasks. The engine automatically finds the critical path and warns about circular dependencies.

03

Simulate risk

Monte Carlo runs 10,000 scenarios against your timeline. See P50/P90 delivery dates before you commit to stakeholders.

04

Execute & track

Assign tasks, track velocity, and get burndown charts in real time. Retrospective data feeds back into future simulations.
