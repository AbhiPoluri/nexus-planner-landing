How it works

Plan smarter, ship faster.

From goal to execution in minutes — not a week of Jira config.

01

Define your goal

Enter a high-level objective. Nexus breaks it into tasks, estimates scope, and flags ambiguous requirements.

02

Map dependencies

Drag to connect tasks. The engine automatically finds the critical path and warns about circular dependencies.

03

Simulate risk

Monte Carlo runs 10,000 scenarios against your timeline. See delivery probability before committing.

04

Execute & track

Assign tasks, track velocity, and get burndown charts in real time. Retrospective data feeds future simulations.
